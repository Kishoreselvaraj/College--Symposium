Just A Min

Non-Technical Events

This helps to analyse how fluent you are in English and how confident you are to communicate and face the challenges on the spot.you are given an on-the-spot title to speak fluently for 2 min

Eligibility:

All UG Engineering students can join.

Rules:

  • Participants have the opportunity to deliver imprompt speeches lasting one or two minutes.
  • Topics for the speeches will be given on the spot, challenging participants to think on their feet.
  • The focus is on imprompt communication, allowing participants to showcase their ability to construct coherent and engaging speeches on the fly.
