Paper Presentation

Technical Events

This event serves as a forum for disseminating in-depth knowledge on a particular topic, facilitating the exchange of information among participants.

Eligibility:

All UG Engineering students cab join.

Rules:

  • Each paper presentation team should have a maximum of four members.
  • Allotted time for the presentation is 15 minutes.
  • An additional 5 minutes are designated for the question and answer session.
  • The maximum number of slides permitted for the presentation is 15.
