Technical Events

A knowledge challenge where you answer questions about specific subjects, like electrical and electronics, to see how much you know. It's like a fun game to test your smarts!

Eligibility:

Any UG engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • Maximum of 2 members per team.
  • Event consists of two different rounds as follows: Round 1 : TechTrek Challenge: A Quest for Knowledge Round 2 : brain freeze
  • There will be certain time span for each round.
  • Respective college ID's are compulsory.
