TRIVIA

Non-Technical Events

Trivia is like a fun game where you answer Questions about random facts (or) Knowledge. It is a chance to test what you know and learn new things in a relaxed and enjoyable way. Think of trivia as a delightful mental challenge, where you dive into a sea of random facts and emerge victorious, armed with knowledge and a smile.

Eligibility:

Any UG engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • A Team can have maximum upto 2 members.
  • 15 seconds for every questions.
  • Don't use mobile phones.
  • questions are asked commonly, Answers are to be submitted after every questions by teams.
  • Arguments (or) opposing with organizing team are not encouraged.
  • participants should bring their respective college ID card.
