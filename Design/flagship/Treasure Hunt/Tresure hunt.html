ADVENTURE UNLEASHED

Flagship Events

Treasure hunts, whether outdoor, virtual or hybrid, are an excellent way to boost team energy and trust. With our team, your group can enjoy a fun, competitive activity together. Nothing compares to the feeling of cracking codes and solving puzzles with teammates to emerge victorious. Be quick on your feet, locate the next clue and take home the win!

Eligibility:

Any UG Engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • Adventure Unleashed will be organised based on the theme “PIRATES OF THE CARIBBEAN”.
  • Maximum 4 members per team
  • Time limit is available.
  • Team must follow all rules and regulations provided by the event organizers.
