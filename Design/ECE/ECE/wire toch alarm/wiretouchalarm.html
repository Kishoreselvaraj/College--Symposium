Wire Touch Alarm

Non-Technical Events

Get ready to spark innovation and explore the world of tactile technology in our Wire Touch Alarm challenge! Join us for an exciting event where participants will design and build their wire touch alarms, showcasing their skills in Electrical and Communication Engineering (ECE).

Eligibility:

All UG Engineering students can join.

Rules:

  • Individual participant.
  • Only one chance is given.
  • Winners will be awarded with prizes.
  • Designers should bring their own laptop.
