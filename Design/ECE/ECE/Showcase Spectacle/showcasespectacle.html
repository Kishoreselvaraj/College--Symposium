Showcase Spectacle

Technical Events

Prepare to be dazzled by the brilliance of innovation at our Showcase Spectacle event in Electrical and Communication Engineering (ECE)! Join us for an evening of technological wonders, cutting-edge projects, and awe-inspiring demonstrations that push the boundaries of what's possible in the world of ECE.

Eligibility:

All UG Engineering students can join.

Rules:

  • Maximum of 4 students per project will be allowed.
  • The model should be in a good condition and the students must bring their necessary accessories.
  • General purpose equipment such as power supply will be provided.
  • Prizes will be provided as per the judgement..
