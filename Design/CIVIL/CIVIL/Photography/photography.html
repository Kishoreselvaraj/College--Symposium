Photography

Non-Technical Events

Photography is the art of capturing moments, emotions, and beauty through the lens of a camera. It allows individuals to express their creativity, tell stories, and evoke powerful emotions with a single image. From breathtaking landscapes to intimate portraits, photography offers a window into different perspectives and experiences. Through composition, lighting, and technique, photographers craft visual masterpieces that inspire and resonate with audiences worldwide.

  • Any UG engineering students can Join.
