Best Manager

Technical Events

In this event the participant's administrative, managing and decision making skills are evaluated and the participant who meets all the skills will be declared as the best manager.

Eligibility:

All students can join.

Rules:

  • 1 member per team
  • 150 rupees for each person
  • Total number of rounds: 3
  • Each participants should carry their updated resume
  • Judges decision will be final.
