IPL Auction

non Technical Events

In this event, questions will be asked based on the year (2023). Participants will be provided with a virtual wallet. The team will participate in a virtual auction and have to form a team of 10 players. Team which contains the best players will be the winner.

Eligibility:

All students can join.

Rules:

  • 2 members per team
  • 150 rupees for each person
  • Total number of rounds: 3
