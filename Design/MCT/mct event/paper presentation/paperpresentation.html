TECHVISION SUMMIT

Technical Events

Are you ready to showcase your ideas on the big stage? Join us to get an exclusive platform to bring out your unique ideas and insights into cutting edge topics in the field of tech. Oh wait, these ideas should be original by the way !.

Eligibility:

Any UG Engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • Paper should follow IEEE norms.
  • Each team should consist of two participants, not more than that.
  • Abstract should not exceed more than 250 words.
  • Time duration of the presentation will be 10 minutes i.e. 8 minutes for presenting and 2 minutes for questioning by judges.
  • Participants must bring the printout of their abstract and the presentation.
