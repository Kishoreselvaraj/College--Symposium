CADapalooza

Technical Events

The event is to test the creativity and drafting skills of the participants in AUTOCAD. Design the best Engineering products with your timing sense to encounter the opponents.

Eligibility:

Any UG Engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • Individual event.
  • Time Duration – 30 Minutes.
  • Consists of following rounds :
    • Round 1 - MCQ.
    • Round 2 - Designing.
    • Total Scores will be evaluated for these events and final results will be declared.
