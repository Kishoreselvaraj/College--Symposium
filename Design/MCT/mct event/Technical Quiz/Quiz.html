Technical Events

The Technical Quiz event aims to test participants' knowledge in various fields of technology, including but not limited to Computer Science, Electronics and Information Technology. The event is designed to challenge the participants understanding of technical concepts, problem-solving abilities and critical thinking skills.

Eligibility:

Any UG Engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • Each team consists of 2 members
  • Consists of following rounds :
    • Round 1 - General Aptitude.
    • Round 2 - Current affairs.
    • Round 3 - Finals.
