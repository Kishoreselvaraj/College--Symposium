Mr Welder

Technical Events

The platform to exhibit your creativity through welded joints participants will be given with a few workpieces of same size and they have to make a creative and meaningful components using arc welding process within the stipulated time.

Eligibility:

UG students ( Mechanical) can participate.

Rules:

  • Individual participant
  • Safety guidelines will be given to the participants
  • The participants should know to weld the workpiece.
  • The participants should bring their college ID card.
