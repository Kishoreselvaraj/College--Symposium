Non-Technical Events

An escape room event offers a thrilling and immersive experience where participants are challenged to solve puzzles and riddles to escape within a set time limit.Teams must work together, communicate effectively, and think creatively to uncover clues, unlock hidden secrets, and ultimately break free from the room.

Eligibility:

UG students ( Mechanical) can participate.

Rules:

  • The team can have maximum 2 members.
  • Participants should bring their college ID.
