Master Mind

Non-Technical Events

Master Mind is an engaging and interactive code-breaking game that challenges participants' problem-solving skills. Guests can compete individually or in teams to crack the code and become the ultimate Master Mind. This classic game adds a fun and competitive element to any event, keeping guests entertained and engaged. With its focus on deduction and strategy, Master Mind is sure to captivate players of all ages. Bring the excitement of Master Mind to your event and watch as guests test their wits and enjoy a thrilling experience.

Eligibility:

UG students ( Mechanical) can participate.

Rules:

  • The team can have maximum 4 members.
  • Participants should bring their college ID.
