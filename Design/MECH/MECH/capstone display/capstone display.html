Technical Events


 It is a platform where the participating teams or Individual can showcase their projects and ideas. It is a chance to show off your hard work, explain your ideas and convince the value of your project.

Eligibility:

UG students ( Mechanical) can participate.

Rules:

  • The topic can be of your interest,but it should be connected to the field of Mechanical Engineering.
  • The abstract and the gesture of the project are to be sent. The abstract should be brief, concise and contain the overview of the project .Teams will be shortlisted based on their abstract only.
  • Send your abstract to - (frictofest24ciet@gmail.com)
  • The selected Team will be requested to bring the prototype or the working model.
  • The file name should be the title of your project.
  • The subject of the mail should be (Project Presentation) followed by your paper title .Eg project presentation - compressed air engine
  • Teams can have maximum of 4 members.
  • Format:
    #Title of the project
    #Abstract
    #Introduction
    #Objective
    #Methology
    #Conclusion
  • The decision of the judges will be final.
  • Participants should bring their respective College ID card.
