Codebugs

Technical Events

In the CodeBug challenge, participants will engage in a series of dynamic tasks that test their programming knowledge and problem-solving skills. First up, contenders will dive into a Programming Quiz. Next, the challenge takes a twist with an Error Debugging session. Finally, the challenge crescendos with a thrilling exercise where participants are provided with partial code and output. The CodeBug challenge promises to be a thrilling journey through the intricate world of programming, where only the most skilled and adaptable coders will emerge victorious.

Eligibility:

Any UG engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • Team can contain maximum 2 members or participate as individual.
  • Members from diffrent college can form a team
  • Winners are selected based on the points.
  • coders should bring their own laptop.
  • Online browsing team will be eliminated.
