Web Master Challenge

Technical Events

Web Master Challenges could cover a wide range of aspects related to web development, design, content management, and overall website optimization.Participants may be given specific tasks or objectives to complete within a given time frame.

Eligibility:

Any UG engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • Team can contain maximum 2 members or participate as individual.
  • Members from different college can form a team.
  • Topics are given on the spot.
  • Participant should bring laptop and other accessories.
  • Online browsing or copy of codes are prohibited.
