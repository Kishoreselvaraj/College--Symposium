Motif Design

Non-Technical Events

Craft a comprehensive design brief that includes information about your organization, target audience, preferred colors, style preferences, and any specific elements you want in the logo. The more details you provide, the better the designers can understand your vision.

Eligibility:

Any UG engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • Participant should design a logo within a given time frame.
  • Participant should bring laptop and other accessories.
  • Team can contain maximum 2 members or participate as individual.
  • Provide guidelines for the use of text in logos, including font choices.
  • Submissions must be original creations of the participants.
  • Copying or referring a logo in online are prohibited.
