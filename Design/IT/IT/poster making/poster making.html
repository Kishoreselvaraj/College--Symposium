Poster making

Non-Technical Events

Welcome to the Poster-Making Competition! This exciting event is designed to showcase your creativity and design skills.Whether you're a seasoned designer or a budding artist, this competition provides a platform for you to express your ideas and make a visual impact.

Eligibility:

Any UG engineering students can Join.

Rules:

  • Topics are given on the spot.
  • Participant should design a poster within a given time frame.
  • Participant should bring laptop and other accessories.
  • A team can contain maximum of 2 members.
  • Submissions must be a original creations of the participants.
  • Copying or referring a poster in online are prohibited.
